There is this topic about using AI and creating your second brain, I am unsure why people want to do that. It sounds so egocentric? If I'm Einstein, sure. Why would I want to clone MY brain?

I rather create an agent that is my opposite in everyway so it can fill in my gaps, check my blindspots and make my thinking and execution more robust. So I did, I feel invincible in thinking now. 😆
