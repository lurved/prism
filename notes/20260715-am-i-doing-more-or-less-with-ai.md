Another reflection point: do I do more or less with AI, given all this talk about AI taking over jobs, and even the prophecies about the extinction of the human species?

Larger question aside, historical evidence suggests that most species has its time and space to evolve or to end. Nothing is forever. This isn't meant to scare anyone, it's just accepting that change is part of change. So, is AI a friend or a foe? My take is that it's a new friend with an unclear agenda, and even if it turns out to be an enemy, let's keep it close. Wishing it away won't do anything. It's here.

My current verdict is that I'm doing more with AI. Thinking more, creating more, doing more. And if a "job" gets automated, there are more jobs to be done. Work itself is being redefined, yet as a singular entity, I'm feeling invigorated.
